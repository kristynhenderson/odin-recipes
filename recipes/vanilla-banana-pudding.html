Vanilla Banana Pudding

Description

A creamy dessert with a hint of vanilla. Combine banana cream pudding mix, milk, whipped cream, sliced bananas, and crushed Nilla Wafers in a large bowl. Place in fridge until ready to serve. Scoop up a spoonful of crunchy vanilla wafer cookies and silky banana cream pudding. Enjoy a fruity dessert perfect for family gatherings!

Ingredients

  • Sliced bananas
  • Crushed Nilla Wafers
  • Milk
  • Instant banana cream pudding mix
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Stir together instant pudding mix and milk in a large bowl.
  2. Fold in the whipped cream.
  3. Add sliced bananas and crushed Nilla Wafers.
  4. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
