Vanilla Banana Pudding

Description

Ingredients

  • Sliced bananas
  • Crushed Nilla Wafer Cookies
  • Milk
  • Instant banana cream pudding mix
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Pour lime Jell-O into medium mixing bowl.
  2. Add marshmallows. Slice cream cheese into small chunks (quarter-sized or smaller) and add to marshmallows and Jell-O.
  3. Add one cup of boiling water, stir thoroughly. The marshmallows and cream cheese should begin to melt.
  4. Add second cup of boiling water after one minute of stirring and continue to stir thoroughly. The cream cheese and marshmallows should be mostly melted, but not completely.
