Lime Sherbet Punch

Description

A sugary punch with a citrus aftertaste. Combine frosty lime sherbet and bubbly ginger ale in a punch bowl. Let the sherbet begin to melt before serving for the best flavor profile. Pour a cup of milky lime sherbet and peppery ginger ale. Enjoy a refreshing punch perfect for special occasions!

Ingredients

  • 1 tub of lime sherbet
  • 1 2-liter bottle of ginger ale

Steps

  1. Grab a punch bowl.
  2. Spoon sherbet into punch bowl.
  3. Pour ginger ale over sherbet and stir.
  4. Let sherbet begin to melt before serving.
