Lime Jell-O Fluff

Description

A sweet dessert with just the right amount of zest. Combine lime Jell-O, mini marshmallows, cream cheese, crushed pineapple, and boiling water in a medium bowl and stir thoroughly. Pour the mixture into a pan and let it sit in the fridge. Once enough time has passed, sink your teeth into a smooth, creamy texture complimented with fluffy marshmallows. Enjoy a sweet and sour dessert perfect for the holidays!

Ingredients

  • 1 large box (6oz) lime Jell-O
  • 1 bag (10oz) mini marshmallows
  • 1 block (8oz) cream cheese (room temperature)
  • 1 can (20oz) crushed pineapple
  • 2 cups boiling water

Steps

  1. Pour lime Jell-O into medium mixing bowl.
  2. Add marshmallows. Slice cream cheese into small chunks (quarter-sized or smaller) and add to marshmallows and Jell-O.
  3. Add one cup of boiling water, stir thoroughly. The marshmallows and cream cheese should begin to melt.
  4. Add second cup of boiling water after one minute of stirring and continue to stir thoroughly. The cream cheese and marshmallows should be mostly melted, but not completely.

  6. Pour into 9x11 pan and place in fridge to set for two hours.
